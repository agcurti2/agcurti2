This is a work in progress. I am building a swarm of 50 quadrotors and the required support infrastructure for a swarm management system. The individual quadrotors have been optimized for maximum flight time and payload capacity. Each quadrotor also has independent onboard computation, precise localization, peer-to-peer communication, and autonomous control. The swarm management system handles hands-free operations such as robot recharging, uploading code, downloading flight results, running experiments, and turing robots on and off.
